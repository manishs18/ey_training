3. LegalTech – ContractiQ
Choice: Hybrid Multi-Agent Architecture
Justification

The workflow naturally splits into two phases.

Phase 1 (Parallel)

Each of the 800 contracts can be analyzed independently:

Clause extraction
Obligation detection
Risk identification

This is embarrassingly parallel.

Phase 2 (Global Synthesis)

Results must then be combined to:

Find cross-contract dependencies
Detect cascading change-of-control clauses
Produce executive risk summary
Generate Red/Amber/Green heatmap

This requires a coordinating synthesis agent.
