1. E-Commerce / Retail – ShopIQ
Personalized Product Recommendation Email
Choice: Single Agent
Justification

This workflow operates on a single shared user context:

Purchase history
Browsing history
Recommendation model output
Business rules
Email generation

Every step depends on the same context object and executes in a strict sequential order.

Introducing multiple agents would require:

Context serialization/deserialization
Inter-agent communication
Additional orchestration overhead

With 4 million users processed nightly and an SLA of <3 seconds per user, minimizing latency is more important than specialization.

Therefore, a single orchestrating agent with multiple internal tools is the optimal architecture.
