2. Healthcare / Clinical AI – Apollo Diagnostics
Choice: Multi-Agent
Justification

Each stage belongs to a completely different medical domain and requires specialized tools.

Step	Domain	Tool
CT Interpretation	Radiology AI	Imaging model
Contraindication Check	Clinical Decision Support	Medication database
Follow-up Scheduling	Hospital Operations	EMR APIs
Patient Communication	Medical NLP	Letter generator

Each stage has:

Different permissions
Different knowledge base
Different failure modes
Validation gates before proceeding

Failure isolation is critical because an incorrect diagnosis should prevent downstream scheduling or communication.

A Multi-Agent architecture provides:

Modularity
Human approval checkpoints
Better auditability
Independent upgrades
